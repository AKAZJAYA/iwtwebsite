Q. How can I schedule a vaccination appointment?

A. You can schedule an appointment through this portal by selecting your preferred vaccination center, date, and time. Follow the instructions provided to complete the booking process.

Q. Can I choose which vaccine I receive?

A. The availability of vaccine types may vary. You will be provided with information on the available vaccines at the vaccination center you select, and you can make your choice based on that information.



Q. Are there any side effects of the vaccine?

A. Common side effects include pain at the injection site, mild fever, and fatigue. These side effects are generally short-lived and indicate that the body is building immunity.
